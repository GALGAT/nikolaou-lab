Research Areas

Our lab focuses on solving complex engineering problems through computational modeling and data science.

Intelligent Systems & Feedback Control

Developing fundamental methods for the intelligent design and operation of engineered systems. We utilize mathematical modeling, optimization, statistics, and feedback loops to improve safety, reliability, and performance across various industries, from microchip fabrication to snack food production.

Effective Development and Use of Antibiotics

Designing clinical practices that maximize the killing effect of antibiotics on infectious bacteria while minimizing toxicity for human patients. This highly interdisciplinary work bridges engineering and medicine to optimize dosing strategies.

Sustainable Energy Solutions

Addressing critical global challenges through the generation of clean energy and the development of efficient, sustainable energy storage systems.

Applied Research & Industry Collaboration

Collaborating with field practitioners to test fundamental solutions on real-world problems. Solutions are validated using computer simulations, laboratory experiments, or full-scale tests in sectors ranging from oil and gas and refining to pharmaceuticals and semiconductor manufacturing.
