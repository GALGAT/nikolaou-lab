About Dr. Michael Nikolaou

Dr. Michael Nikolaou is a Professor of Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering at the University of Houston. His research group focuses on the application of systems engineering principles to a diverse range of complex problems.

Current research efforts span from process control and optimization in the energy sector to predictive pharmacokinetic and pharmacodynamic (PK/PD) modeling. By leveraging machine learning, scientific computing, and transport phenomena, the lab develops multi-scale models to optimize processes and medical treatments.
